Design Notes:

  • DISABLED: Grey speaker with separate X lines that don't overlap the speaker body
  • ENABLED: Gold colored simple speaker (no X, no waves) - clearly "on"
  • SPEAKING: Gold speaker with animated sound waves extending outward
  • The X in disabled state is now positioned at coordinates (23,9) to (17,15) - well away from speaker
